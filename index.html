About Me

Hi, I’m Nihal Joshi, a creative and detail-oriented full-stack web developer passionate about transforming ideas into immersive digital experiences. With 2+ years of professional experience , I specialize in building everything from personal portfolios to scalable professional platforms such as eCommerce stores, business solutions, and custom applications.

My goal is not just to make websites look great but to ensure they perform flawlessly, adapt seamlessly, and deliver a premium user experience.

Beyond coding, I thrive on continuous learning, creative problem-solving, and exploring new technologies that push boundaries. Whether it’s designing smooth UI animations, optimizing performance, or shaping intuitive user journeys, I’m dedicated to creating digital products that stand out and leave an impact.
